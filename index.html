About

A product of rose and love, Rhoda and Amor, I was born in the Diving Capital of the Philippines, Zambales, on the 19th of April 1997. I grew up in Olongapo City where I finished my Primary and Secondary education. As of now, I'm pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology with majors in Web Technology at University of the Cordilleras, Baguio City.

My hobbies include running... a blog.
I also enjoy programming and graphic designing. I love anime as much as I love dogs and puppies. Not to mention, I also draw.
And oh, I'm really fond of playing video games and reading books during my leisure time.
